The High-Level Advisory Board on Effective
Multilateralism (HLAB) was established by the
United Nations Secretary-General and builds
on Our Common Agenda, a report released by
Secretary-General António Guterres in September
2021 that calls for stronger governance of key
issues of global concern. The HLAB is co-chaired
by EllenJohnson Sirleaf, former President of
Liberia, and Stefan Löfven, former Prime Minister
of Sweden, and supported by the United Nations
University Centre for Policy Research (UNU-CPR).
