The MDBs share key core governance characteristics: pre-dominantly (or exclusively) sovereign shareholders and voting power within the institutions based mostly on shareholding. Beyond this, governance varies in a number of ways.
First, the composition of the largest shareholders varies considerably across MDBs, although the United States, more frequently than any other country, is either the largest or sec- ond largest shareholder.
