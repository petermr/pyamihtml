The South Centre is the intergovernmental organization of developing countries that helps developing countries to combine their efforts and expertise to promote their common interests in the international are- na. The South Centre was established by an Intergovernmental Agree-ment which came into force on 31 July 1995. Its headquarters is in Geneva, Switzerland.
