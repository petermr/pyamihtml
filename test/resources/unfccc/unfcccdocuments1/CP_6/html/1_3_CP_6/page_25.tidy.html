Resolution 3/CP.6
Expression of gratitude to the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands and to the City and people of The Hague
The Conference of the Parties,
Having met in The Hague from 13 to 25 November 2000 at the invitation of the Government of the Netherlands,
1. Expresses its profound gratitude to the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands for having made it possible for the sixth session of the Conference of the Parties to be held in The Hague;
2. Requests the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to convey to the City and the people of The Hague the gratitude of the Conference of the Parties for the hospitality and warm welcome extended to the participants.
9thplenary meeting 25 November 2000
