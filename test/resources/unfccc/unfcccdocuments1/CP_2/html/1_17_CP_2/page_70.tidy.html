5.The Geneva Ministerial Declaration
At its 7th plenary meeting, on 18 July 1996, the Conference of the Parties took note of the Geneva Ministerial Declaration and agreed that it should be annexed to the report of the Conference (see Part One, section IV, paras. 41 and 45, of the present report). For the text of the Declaration, see the annex below. For statements made in this connection, see Part One, annex IV, of the present report.
6.Calendar of meetings
At its 9th plenary meeting, on 19 July 1996, the Conference of the Parties, noting the recommendation of the Bureau that the SBSTA, the SBI and the Ad Hoc Group on Article 13 should not meet concurrently with the third session of the Conference of the Parties, agreed to the following calendar of meetings proposed by the Executive Secretary on the basis of discussions in the Bureau. The Conference also agreed that the Bureau would keep the calendar under review. (See Part One, section II G, para. 25, of the present report).
