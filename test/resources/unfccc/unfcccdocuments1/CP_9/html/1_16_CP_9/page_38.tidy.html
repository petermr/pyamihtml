(1)Parties do not have to prepare estimates for categories contained in appendices 3a.2, 3a.3 and 3a.4 of the IPCC good practice guidance for LULUCF, although they may do so if they wish.
(2) Land categories may be further divided according to climate zones, management system, soil type, vegetation type, tree species, ecological zones or national land classification.
