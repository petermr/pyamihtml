C. Criteria
The effectiveness of the financial mechanism will be assessed taking into account the following:
(a) The transparency of decision-making processes;
(b) The adequacy, predictability and timely disbursement of funds for activities indeveloping country Parties;
(c) The responsiveness and efficiency of the GEF project cycle and expeditedprocedures, including its operational strategy, as they relate to climate change;
(d) The amount of resources provided to developing country Parties, includingfinancing for technical assistance and investment projects;
(e) The amount of finance leveraged;
(f) The sustainability of funded projects.
