8.Recommends that the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol, at its first session, adopt a decision endorsing a framework for capacity building under the Convention that parallels the framework contained in the annex below, with additional reference to priority areas for capacity building relating to implementation of the Kyoto Protocol;
9.Requests the secretariat, consistent with Article 8 of the Convention:
(a)To cooperate with multilateral and bilateral institutions to facilitate the implementation of this framework;
(b)To collect, process, compile and disseminate the information needed by the Conference of the Parties and the subsidiary bodies to monitor progress in the implementation of this framework.
