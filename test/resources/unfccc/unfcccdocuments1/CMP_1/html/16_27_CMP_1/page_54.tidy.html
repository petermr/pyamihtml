2. Annual review
15. Each Party included in Annex I shall be subject to an annual review of:
(a) The annual inventory, including the national inventory report and the common reporting format (CRF), for conformity with Article 5, paragraph 2, in accordance with the procedures contained in part II of these guidelines
(b) The following supplementary information, in accordance with the guidelines for the preparation of the information required under Article 7, section I:
(i) Information provided during the commitment period for land use, land-use change and forestry activities under Article 3, paragraphs 3 and 4, for conformity with the requirements of relevant decisions of the COP/MOP, in accordance with the procedures contained in part II of these guidelines
(ii) Information on assigned amounts pursuant to Article 3, paragraphs 7 and 8, emission reduction units, certified emission reductions, assigned amount units and removal units, in accordance with the procedures contained in part III of these guidelines
(iii) Changes in national systems in accordance with the procedures contained in part IV of these guidelines
(iv) Changes in national registries in accordance with the procedures contained in part V of these guidelines
(v) Information provided on matters relating to Article 3, paragraph 14, and supplementary information in accordance with the procedures contained in part VI of these guidelines.
