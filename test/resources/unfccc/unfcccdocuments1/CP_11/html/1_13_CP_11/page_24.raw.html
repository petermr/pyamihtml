Decides that the meetings of each of the Consultative Group of Experts on National Communications from Parties not included in Annex I to the Convention, the Expert Group on Technology Transfer and the Least Developed Countries Expert Group to be financed from the core budget shall number not more than three per biennium, bearing in mind that additional meetings will be financed from the Trust Fund for Supplementary Activities;
