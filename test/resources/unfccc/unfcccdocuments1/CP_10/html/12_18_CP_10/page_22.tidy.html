The review of the report upon expiration of the additional period for fulfilling commitments and of the information submitted in accordance with paragraph 116of the annex to decision -/CMP.1 (Article 7) shall be completed within 14 weeks of the due date for the submission of the information. The expert review team shall prepare a draft report within eight weeks of the due date for submission of the information.
