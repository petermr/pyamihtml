Each Party included in Annex I shall report on any actions and the date of such actions taken to correct any problem that caused a discrepancy to occur, any changes to the national registry to prevent a discrepancy from reoccurring, and the resolution of any previously identified questions of implementation pertaining to transactions.

11. Each Party included in Annex I shall, for the year of submission of the annual inventory for the last year of the commitment period, report the supplementary information described in this section of the guidelines that relates to the accounting of assigned amounts for that commitment period, in conjunction with the report upon expiration of the additional period for fulfilling commitments referred to in paragraph 49 of the annex to decision -/CMP.1 (Modalities for the accounting of assigned amounts).
