ANNEX II
Tasks of the review of first communications from Annex I Parties
The review process should undertake six major tasks:
1.Review key qualitative information and quantitative data points contained in national communications;
2.Review policies and measures described in national communications;
3.Assess the information contained in national communications against Convention commitments, and assess the extent to which progress towards the objective of the Convention is being achieved;
4.Describe expected progress in the limitation of emissions by sources and enhancement of removals by sinks of greenhouse gases, on the basis of information contained in national communications;
5.Describe expected progress in cooperation to prepare for adaptation;
6.Aggregate data across national communications with respect to inventories, projections, effects of measures and financial transfers, but without adding up the individual national totals for projections and the effects of measures.
