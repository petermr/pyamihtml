Decision 17/CP.1
Adoption of the Convention budget for the biennium 1996-1997
The Conference of the Parties,
Recalling that paragraph 4 of the financial procedures for the Conference of the Parties1/provides that the Conference shall adopt a budget by consensus prior to the commencement of the financial period that it covers,
