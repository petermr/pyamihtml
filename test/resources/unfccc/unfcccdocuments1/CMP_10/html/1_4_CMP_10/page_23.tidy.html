Decision 6/CMP.10
Synergy relating to accreditation under the mechanisms of the Kyoto Protocol
The Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol,
Recalling decision 6/CMP.8, paragraph 15(b),
1. Requests the Executive Board of the clean development mechanism and the Joint Implementation Supervisory Committee to collaborate in considering the establishment of a joint accreditation committee under their authority and supervision and in the operation of accreditation, within the mandates established by decisions 3/CMP.1 and 9/CMP.1 and other relevant decisions of the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol related to accreditation;
2. Also requests the Executive Board of the clean development mechanism and the Joint Implementation Supervisory Committee to report on the progress of the actions referred to in paragraph 1 above to the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol at its eleventh session (Novemberâ€“December 2015).
