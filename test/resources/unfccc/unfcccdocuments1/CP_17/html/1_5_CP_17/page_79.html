XV. Amendments to the rules of procedure
61. These rules of procedure may be amended by the TEC by consensus and, to be effective, must be approved formally by the COP. Pending formal approval, the TEC may decide to apply the amendment provisionally.
XVI. Overriding authority of the Convention
62. In the event of any conflict between any provision of these rules and any provision of the Convention, the Convention shall take precedence.
