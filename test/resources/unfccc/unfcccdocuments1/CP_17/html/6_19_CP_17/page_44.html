Common reporting format tables
Owing to the complexity of and the importance of colour coding in the common reporting format (CRF) tables, they are not included in this document but can be downloaded from the UNFCCC website, both as an Excel file and in pdf format, at <http://unfccc.int/national_reports/annex_i_ghg_inventories/reporting_requirements/items/2759.php>.
