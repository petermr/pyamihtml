N.Table 6. Memo item: Corrective transactions undertaken in the reported year
46. In tables 6(a) to 6(c), Annex I Parties shall report any corrective transactions undertaken in the reported year relating to previously reported years, including transactions to address a correction to the compilation and accounting database applied by the Compliance Committee, pursuant to paragraph 5(b) in chapter V of the annex to decision 27/CMP.1. It should be noted that quantities of Kyoto Protocol units reported here are included in the annual transactions reported in tables 2 and 3 and are reported in tables 6(a) to 6(c) as a memo item for the purpose of transparency. Parties shall provide explanations for these transactions in accompanying text, as required by paragraph 8 of section E of the guidelines for reporting under Article 7 of the Kyoto Protocol.
