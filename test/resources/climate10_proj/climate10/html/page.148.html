The AFOLU sector offers significant near - term mitigation potential at relatively low cost and can

provide 20 - 30% of the 2050 emissions reduction described in scenarios that likely limit warming

to 2°C or lower ( high evidence, medium agreement ). The AFOLU sector can provide 20 – 30%

(interquartile range) of the global mitigation needed for a 1.5 o C or 2 o C pathway towards 2050, though

there are highly variable mitigation strategies for how AFOLU potential can be deployed for achieving

climate target s { Illustrative Mitigation Pathways in 7.5} . T he estimated economic (< USD100 tCO 2 - eq -

1 ) AFOLU sector mitigation potential is 8 to 14 GtCO 2 - eq yr - 1 between 2020 - 2050, with the bottom end

of this range representing the mean from IAMs and the upper end representing the mean estimate from

global sectoral studies. The economic potential is about half of the technical potential from AFOLU,
