Sánchez-González et al. [33,40] established that the essential oil of Melaleuca alternifolia, also known as tea tree essential oil (TTO) is composed of terpene, mainly monoterpenes, sesquiterpenes, and tertiary alcohols [33,40,41,42,43]. The main components of TTO are terpineol, cineol, pinen, and terpinen and demonstrated antimicrobial activity [40,44].
