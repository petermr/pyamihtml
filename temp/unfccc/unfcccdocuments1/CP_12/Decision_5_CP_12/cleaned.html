Decision 5/CP.12
Development and transfer of technologies
The Conference of the Parties,
1. Decides to extend the Expert Group on Technology Transfer for one year including its current membership;
2. Decides to forward the text of a draft decision,1for consideration by the Subsidiary Body for Scientific and Technological Advice, at its twenty-sixth session, with a view to adopting a decision on this matter at the thirteenth session of the Conference of the Parties.
7thplenary meeting 17 November 2006
1The text of the draft decision (all in square brackets) is contained in the report of the SBSTA on its twenty-fifth session (FCCC/SBSTA/2006/11, annex II).
