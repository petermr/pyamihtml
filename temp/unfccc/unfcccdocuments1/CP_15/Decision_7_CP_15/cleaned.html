Decision 7/CP.15
Additional guidance to the Global Environment Facility
The Conference of the Parties,
Noting the report of the Global Environment Facility to the Conference of the Parties,1
Recalling its decision to provide annual guidance to the Global Environment Facility in accordance with the annex to decision 12/CP.2,
Requests the Subsidiary Body for Implementation to continue its consideration of additional guidance to the Global Environment Facility at its thirty-second session, with a view to recommending a draft decision for adoption by the Conference of the Parties at its sixteenth session.
