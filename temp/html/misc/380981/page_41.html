his chapter offers an introduction to
Working Group 1 of the International
Science and Evidence based Education (ISEE)
Assessment. It begins by establishing a basic
claim on which the Assessment builds: the need
to mobilize education to support flourishing.
After describing this foundational claim, we
outline the structure of Working Group 1 and
explain the role of each of the subsequent four
chapters within it.
