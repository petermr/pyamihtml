2 The UNEP’s annual assessment of the global 2030 ‘emission gap’ between aggregated country NDCs and specific target mitigation\npathways (UNEP 2020), is only affected to a limited degree. This latter\nprocedure, however, is agnostic to the reasons for the observed mismatch, and often uses a constant offset. The adjustment described\nhere allows this mismatch to be resolved by drawing on a scientific understanding of the underlying reasons, and thus provides a more\ninformed and accurate basis for estimating the emission gap.
